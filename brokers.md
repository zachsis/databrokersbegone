## State Privacy Law Reference

| State | Law | Key Rights | Opt-Out Deadline |
|-------|-----|-----------|-----------------|
| California | CCPA (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.100 et seq.) | Deletion (§ 1798.105), Opt-out of sale (§ 1798.120) | 45 days + 45-day extension |
| Oregon | OCPA (ORS 646A.570 et seq.) | Deletion (§ 646A.574), Opt-out of sale/targeting (§ 646A.578) | 45 days + 15-day extension |
| Colorado | CPA (C.R.S. § 6-1-1301 et seq.) | Deletion, Opt-out of sale/targeting | 45 days + 45-day extension |
| Connecticut | CTDPA (Conn. Gen. Stat. § 42-515 et seq.) | Deletion, Opt-out of sale/targeting | 45 days |
| Virginia | VCDPA (Va. Code § 59.1-575 et seq.) | Deletion, Opt-out of sale/targeting | 45 days + 45-day extension |
| Texas | TDPSA (Tex. Bus. & Com. Code § 541 et seq.) | Deletion, Opt-out of sale/targeting | 45 days + 45-day extension |
| Montana | MCDPA (MCA § 30-14-2801 et seq.) | Deletion, Opt-out of sale/targeting | 45 days + 15-day extension |
| Utah | UCPA (Utah Code § 13-61-101 et seq.) | Deletion, Opt-out of sale/targeting | 45 days + 45-day extension |
